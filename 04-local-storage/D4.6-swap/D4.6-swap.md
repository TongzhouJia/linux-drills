# KP D4.6 — Swap Space (partition & file)
**6 tasks · RHEL 10 · server2 as root**

**1.** Check the current swap with `free -m` and `swapon --show`, and save the output to `/root/swap_before.txt`.

**2.** Create a **512 MiB swap file** at `/swapfile`, format it, enable it, and make it persistent in `/etc/fstab`.

**3.** Create a **512 MiB swap partition** on `/dev/sdb` (a new partition), format it with `mkswap`, and enable it.

**4.** Make the swap **partition** persistent by **UUID** in `/etc/fstab`.

**5.** Secure the swap file by setting its permissions to `600`, and confirm `mkswap`/`swapon` raise no insecure-permission warning.

**6.** Cleanly disable and remove the swap **file**: turn it off, remove its fstab line, and delete the file.
