# KP D6.2 — systemd Timers
**6 tasks · RHEL 10 · server1 as root**

**1.** Create a `.service` + `.timer` pair that runs `/usr/local/bin/backup.sh` **every day at 02:00**, and enable the timer.

**2.** Verify the timer is scheduled with `systemctl list-timers` and save the output to `/root/timers.txt`.

**3.** Use `systemd-analyze calendar` to confirm the **next trigger time** of your `OnCalendar` expression.

**4.** Create a timer that runs its service **15 minutes after boot** and then **every 30 minutes** thereafter.

**5.** Make a timer that **catches up** and runs a job that was missed while the machine was powered off — which directive achieves this? Add it.

**6.** Enable and start the timer, then confirm its associated service has run at least once.
