# KP D5.4 — Extend Logical Volumes
**6 tasks · RHEL 10 · server2 as root**

**1.** Extend `/dev/data_vg/data_lv` by **300 MiB** and grow its **xfs** filesystem (online).

**2.** Extend a different LV by adding **50 more extents** and grow its filesystem.

**3.** Extend an **ext4** LV and grow it with `resize2fs`.

**4.** Extend an LV to use **100% of the remaining free space** in the VG in a single command, then grow the filesystem.

**5.** Before extending, confirm the VG has enough free space (`vgs` / `vgdisplay`) and save it to `/root/vgfree.txt`.

**6.** After extending, verify the new size shows in `df -h` and the existing data is intact.
